# Pitch Deck Outline

**Title:** AI-Automated Auto Insurance Claims Platform
**Target Audience:** Mid-size Insurers and Managing General Agents (MGAs)
**Objective:** Secure pilot programs and early adopters for the platform.

## Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Headline:** Revolutionizing Auto Insurance Claims with AI
- **Sub-headline:** Faster, Cheaper, and More Accurate First Notice of Loss (FNOL) Processing
- **Visual:** A sleek, modern split-screen showing a damaged car on one side and a fast-loading mobile app UI on the other.
- **Presenter Name & Title**
- **Date**

## Slide 2: The Problem
- **Headline:** The Current Claims Process is Broken
- **Points:**
  - **Slow:** Manual review takes 3-7 days for a preliminary estimate.
  - **Expensive:** Costs carriers $50-$150 per claim in operational overhead.
  - **Inaccurate:** Human error in damage assessment leads to inflated payouts or unhappy customers.
  - **Poor Customer Experience:** Claimants are left in the dark, waiting days for a response.
- **Visual:** A timeline comparing the slow, manual process to a modern, instant digital experience.

## Slide 3: The Solution
- **Headline:** AI-Powered Straight-Through Processing
- **Points:**
  - **Instant FNOL:** Claimants submit photos and details via a simple web app in under 5 minutes.
  - **Automated Damage Assessment:** Computer vision analyzes photos instantly to identify parts and severity.
  - **Smart Adjudication:** Rule-based engines auto-approve simple claims and route complex ones to human adjusters.
  - **Integrated Payouts:** Direct integration with Stripe for fast, seamless disbursements.
- **Visual:** A high-level flowchart of the automated claim lifecycle (FNOL -> AI -> Adjudication -> Payout).

## Slide 4: Key Capabilities & Technology
- **Headline:** Built for Speed and Accuracy
- **Points:**
  - **Computer Vision (Claude 4.5 Sonnet / YOLOv8):** Accurate damage detection and cost estimation.
  - **Deterministic Rules Engine:** Customizable guardrails for auto-approval (e.g., <$2,000 threshold).
  - **Fraud Detection:** Real-time analysis of metadata, claim history, and photo anomalies.
  - **Modern Stack:** Next.js frontend, FastAPI backend, PostgreSQL database.
- **Visual:** A diagram highlighting the core technologies powering the platform.

## Slide 5: The Impact (Alpha Results)
- **Headline:** Proven Results in Alpha Testing

  - **98% Accuracy:** Damage estimates align closely with manual baseline assessments.
  - **<$5 Cost per Claim:** A massive reduction in operational overhead.
  - **Sub-5 Minute Processing:** From FNOL submission to payout initiation.
